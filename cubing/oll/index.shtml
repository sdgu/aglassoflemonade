The 2LOLL Subset

Here I'll quickly describe 2-look OLL.


2-look OLL involves orienting the top layer edges (making a cross on the top), which reduces the number of cases to 3 + 7 = 10. The following three algorithms will give the cross as long as there are no edges, two edges in 9 and 12 o' clock, and two edges in 3 and 9 o' clock, respectively.

Replacing First Look Algorithms to Learn Full OLL

One of the stuggles of OLL, I've found, is that I might learn a case, but rarely actually encounter it in a solve. As a result, it's easily forgotten. Instead, after I've solidly remembered the first look algorithms, I pick new ones. Initially, I pause and make sure to identify what pieces would need to be reoriented for it to be the actual OLL case. Obviously, this is no good for speedsolving, but now the algorithms are practiced and can be used in the actual case.
