1

"Where to begin?" the Deltoraman mused,
Replying to kiddos galore
"It was long ago so you may be confused."
For many aren't around anymore.

The legend starts on a lazy afternoon
A hero with greatness in store.
He would have a run in with fortune soon
But trouble was straight in view

See, travel by boat was transportation's tune
And usually safety was coo.
For those travelers who were lacking gold,
However, the presence of pirates was due

As they appeared some first-timers stood cold.
Spells were fired toward the hero
But he readied himself, his excitement unable to hold.


2

They came quite swiftly, they flew through the waves
Heralding the Flag of the Cards
He steadied himself, the day ready to save
Wielding only Fortune's shard

(For the record he didn't know what it was)
The travelers he would guard
But in his plan there were a few major flaws
Cards, not the usual type

A vile group that doesn't follow pirate law
Rule 1: Suffering none to slight
Rule 2: Fight the defenders before taking goods
Rule 3: Flag notches display might

Alas the Cards, trade them as slaves they would
Of course going for the others,
Overpower the hero they could.
